About Little Lemon

Based in chicago, Illinois, Liitle Lemon is a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant, focused on traditional recipes servedwith a modern twist. The chefs drews inspiration from italian, Greek, and Turkish culture and have a menu of 12-15 items that they rotate seasonally. The restaurant has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place meal any time of the day.

Little Lemon is owned by two italian brothers, Mario and Adrian, who moved to the United States to pursue their dream of owning a restaurant. To craft the menu, Mario relies on family recipes and his experience as a chef in italy. Adrian does all the marketing for the restaurant and led th effort to expand the menu beyond classic italian to incorporate additional cuisines from the Mediterraneanregion.
